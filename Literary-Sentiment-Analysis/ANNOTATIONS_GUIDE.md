## How to Use Annotations

### Step 1: Analyze Your Text
1. Paste your poem, story, or literary text
2. Click **"Analyze Text"**
3. Wait for results to appear

### Step 2: Open Annotations
1. Click the **"Annotations"** button in the action bar
2. The annotations panel appears below the results
3. Your original text is displayed in a special text box

### Step 3: Select & Annotate
1. **Click and drag** to select any passage (word, phrase, sentence, or paragraph)
2. Click **"Add Note to Selection"** button
3. A popup form appears showing your selected text
4. Type your note/comment in the text area
5. Click **"Save"**

### Step 4: View Your Annotations
- Selected text is now **highlighted in yellow** with an underline
- **Hover over** the highlighted text to see your note in a tooltip
- All annotations are listed below in chronological order

### Step 5: Manage Annotations
- **Delete:** Click the trash icon (ğŸ—‘ï¸) next to any annotation
- **Export:** Click "Export Annotations" to download as .txt file
- **Clear All:** Click "Clear All" to remove all annotations (confirms first)
