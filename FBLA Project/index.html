Frequently Asked Questions

How long are items kept in the system? ‚ñº
Items are automatically removed from the public listing after 7 days if they remain unclaimed. This helps keep the database current and relevant.
What if I found someone's item? ‚ñº
Great! You can file a report describing the item you found. The owner can then search for it and contact you to arrange pickup. You're helping make our community better!
Is my personal information safe? ‚ñº
Yes! Only your name and email are visible to help facilitate contact. We recommend using your school email and arranging pickups in public, supervised areas on campus.
How do I verify ownership of electronics? ‚ñº
For electronics, you'll need to provide verification details like serial numbers, IMEI, specific damage descriptions, or unique settings/wallpapers that only the owner would know.
Can I edit my report after submitting? ‚ñº
Currently, reports cannot be edited after submission. If you need to make changes, you can mark the item as collected and submit a new report with the correct information.
